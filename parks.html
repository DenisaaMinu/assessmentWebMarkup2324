Parks and Recreation

🌲Hoofddorp's parks and recreational facilities offer the perfect escape for families and individuals looking to enjoy the outdoors and stay active. Hoofddorp has lovely parks where you can relax and play. There are big green spaces, fun playgrounds, and places for picnics and barbeques. Great for families but not only!

Haarlemmermeer Bos Park in Hoofddorp

This park is a beautiful and expansive recreational area located in Hoofddorp, the Netherlands.

An ideal spot for new expat families, where you can explore nature, bike around, let the kids play, and join community events.

Graan voor visch park for children

In a neighborhood filled with kids off all ages, this park is a perfect spot for making friends. With swings, basketball courts, and more, it's where kids have a blast together.
